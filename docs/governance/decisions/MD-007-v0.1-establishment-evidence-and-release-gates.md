# MD-007: v0.1 Establishment Evidence and Release Gates

Status: accepted

Date: 2026-07-17

Decision authority: Ezra

Classification: v0.1 establishment evidence class, frozen mechanism gates, and release mechanics

## Context

VoxProof v0.1 targets mechanism validity on real material with explicit human authority. Track 1 code, exploratory real-material mechanism probes, strict skeleton-compatible calibration correspondence evaluation, and qualifying owner-operated FLEURS human-review evidence under MD-007 D8 at repository HEAD `7efe8ba` exist. All ten MD-007 D9 mechanism gates passed on that evidence. v0.1 remains in progress, not established. Factual record: `quality/evaluation.md`.

Without an accepted evidence-class boundary and frozen mechanism gates, later documentation or run outcomes could silently broaden v0.1 into product-effectiveness, general ASR, precision/recall, external-user usability, or detector-effectiveness claims that this version does not authorize.

These gates were frozen on 2026-07-17, before observing the upcoming qualifying human-review result. Outcome observation does not retroactively weaken, replace, or renegotiate them.

This decision does not itself declare v0.1 established. Current status remains **v0.1 in progress, not established** until every gate in this decision is documented as satisfied and an explicit establishment decision is recorded.

Future changes to these frozen v0.1 gates require another accepted Material Decision. They may not be changed by informal documentation edit, run commentary, or post-hoc threshold adjustment.

## Decision

### D7 — v0.1 establishment scope

v0.1 establishes a reproducible correction mechanism with explicit human authority.

v0.1 does **not** establish:

- product effectiveness;
- general ASR quality;
- precision or recall;
- time savings;
- external-user usability;
- detector effectiveness beyond the explicitly bounded mechanism evidence.

Mechanism establishment, product effectiveness, external-user usability, and general ASR claims are distinct classes. v0.1 may authorize only the first.

### D8 — qualifying human evidence

For v0.1, the project owner may act as the real human reviewer, provided that:

- the owner actually listens to the frozen real-speech material;
- every cue in the declared study scope is explicitly adjudicated;
- decisions and any human-final transcript are provenance-bound;
- community/reference text is not silently substituted for human listening or independent human judgment;
- the reviewer identity/role and review procedure are recorded;
- external target-cohort or facilitated-user testing remains deferred to v0.2.

A **real human reviewer** means an actual listening and adjudication act, not text substitution, reference localization alone, or synthetic controls presented as human judgment.

Owner-operated evidence qualifies for v0.1 only under the recorded procedure above. External facilitated/target-cohort testing remains a v0.2 concern.

### D9 — frozen numeric v0.1 mechanism gates

These requirements are frozen before observing the upcoming qualifying human-review result.

All ten requirements must pass.

No averaging, aggregate score, exception, or waiver may conceal a failed invariant.

These are mechanism-establishment gates evaluated on real human-correction evidence. They are **not** product-effectiveness thresholds.

1. 100% of cues in the declared human-review study scope are adjudicated.
2. Zero cues remain unresolved.
3. 100% cue index, start-time, and end-time skeleton preservation.
4. 100% of canonical review decisions are recorded and bound to the reviewed inputs.
5. 100% of accepted alternatives are materialized exactly as decided.
6. 100% of rejected alternatives preserve the source text at their anchors.
7. Zero unauthorized or silent text changes.
8. Repeated `compare` and `evaluate` runs on identical inputs produce byte-identical reports.
9. Every reported summary count recomputes exactly from the detailed records.
10. Candidate yield, correctness observations, and error distributions remain descriptive only:
    - no minimum candidate-yield threshold;
    - no precision/recall threshold;
    - no detector-effectiveness threshold;
    - no product-effectiveness claim.

### D10 — mixed zh-EN fixture

An authorized mixed Traditional-Chinese/ASCII-Latin fixture must exist before tagging v0.1.0.

Its v0.1 purpose is limited to locking:

- Unicode and UTF-8 byte-anchor safety;
- detector eligibility and hard-boundary behavior;
- deterministic ReviewCase and evidence behavior;
- human-authority and materialization behavior;
- unchanged behavior for unsupported/non-eligible forms.

The fixture is not real-speech evidence or product-effectiveness evidence unless it is separately sourced, classified, and governed as such.

### D11 — release mechanics

- The intended release tag is the annotated tag `v0.1.0`.
- Release notes must state scope, evidence, limitations, and explicit non-claims.
- Creating a GitHub Release is optional and must not broaden the authorized claims.
- Tagging is allowed only after every v0.1 establishment gate is documented as satisfied.
- Research ZIPs, seals, and patches remain untracked unless a separate explicit owner decision authorizes committing them.
- Current status remains `v0.1 in progress, not established` until the gates pass and an explicit establishment decision is recorded.
